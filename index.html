About the Artist

My name is Mackenzie, and I'm an artist and software developer based in the city since 2019. Originally from Cumberland, Wisconsin, I was born in Eau Claire.

Although my formal education is in software development, I am a self-taught artist specializing in various metal mediums. Over the past few years, I have explored and honed my skills in working with chicken wire, wire jewelry, wire-wrapped stones, metal clay, and aluminum/steel wire sculpting.

Much of my work is inspired by nature, and I strive to capture its beauty and complexity in my sculptures. With each piece, I aim to create unique and intricate works that reflect the natural world's elegance.
