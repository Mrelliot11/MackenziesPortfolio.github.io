Biography

My name is Mackenzie. I was born in Eau Claire and raised in Cumberland, Wisconsin. I moved to the city with my husband in 2019. Currently I have no formal training in an art-related field, I have been self-taught so far. The degree I hold is in software development. Over the past few years, I've been practicing with different metal mediums. So far, I have tried chicken wire, wire jewelry, wire wrapped stones, metal clay, and aluminum/steel wire sculpting. Nature is inspriational and I enjoy attempting to imitate it through my sculptures.
