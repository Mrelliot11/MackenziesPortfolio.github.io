Biography

My name is Mackenzie Dubrow. I was born in Eau Claire and raised in Cumberland Wisconsin. I moved to the city with my husband in 2019. I have an associates in software development, I currently have no professional training in an art related field. Over the past few years I've been practing with different metal mediums. So far I have tried chicken wire, wire jewerly, metal clay, and alumimum and steel wire sculpting. I try and imitate nature with my sculptures.

About Me

I am a passionate artist who specializes in creating unique metal wire sculptures. Each sculpture is meticulously crafted by hand, resulting in intricate and captivating pieces of art.
